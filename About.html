Nisandi Jayasuriya is an enthusiastic Computer Science Undergraduate at Informatics Institute of Technology which is affiliated with the University of Westminster.

She started her primary in 2003 at Royal Institute International School Nugegoda Branch and finished her secondary education in 2019 at Royal Institute International School Havelock Town Branch. Being a member of the Prefects' Guild of Royal Institute, leadership attitude was brought up in her by fulfilling the duties as a Junior Prefect as well as a Senior Prefect.

She was a talented young athlete at School. She participated for Inter-branch Athletic Championship achieving Girls' Championship in years 2011, 2015 and 2016. She is passionate about art and showcased her creative artistic skills in several Art Exhibitions.
